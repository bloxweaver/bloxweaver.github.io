AI-Driven Content Management and Multilingual Strategies on The Lucent Perspective

David Meikle
|

October 17, 2024

We were delighted to join Rebecca Hastings on her podcast, The Lucent Perspective for a wide ranging conversation covering content management, AI customisation, and preparing companies for exits.

On the podcast our founder, David Meikle, shared his expertise on leveraging AI to tackle multilingual content management, a challenge many global companies face today. He shared a deep dive into “content fabric” and how it is reshaping enterprise content strategies.

It was a wide ranging conversation covering the following main topics:

  • Challenges in multilingual content management
  • AI applications in language technology
  • The concept of “content fabric” for global businesses
  • Customizing AI for enterprise success
  • Benefits of open-source contributions to career development
  • Preparing companies for exits from a CTO’s perspective

Give it a listen if you’re curious about how AI is shaping the future of content, and let us know your thoughts. Thanks again to Rebecca for the great conversation!
