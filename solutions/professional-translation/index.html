Overview

Perfect blend of professional expertise and AI efficiency

Our professional translation service pairs our advanced AI technology with professional content experts to deliver precision content that resonates in any language, exactly as intended

Expert Translators
Professional translators specialized in your industry, backed by our proven five-pillar precision content framework. Our professional content experts bring nuanced understanding of industry-specific terminology, conventions, contextual implications, and above all, a dedication to their craft.
AI Augmentation
Our translation platform provides an easy-to-use interface with adaptive translation models, intelligent context capabilities, in-built support tools, intelligent AI agents, and advanced content processing that reduces manual effort and maximizes quality.
Quality First
With in-built quality checks, glossary, quality estimation/evaluation models, and additional tools to aid content professionals with their work, the platform helps content professionals do their best work.

Key Features

Professional expertise in every project

Built for organizations that demand the highest quality translations with industry specialization, cultural adaptation, and AI-enhanced efficiency.

Industry Specialization
Specialized expertise across a range of domains including legal, medical, technical, financial, and marketing domains. Our translators understand industry-specific terminology, conventions and implications across cultures and markets
Advanced AI
Advanced adaptive translation models learn and improve as you translate, quality evaluation models that help detect errors or optimise review, and AI Agents such as Context Agents can dynamically query knowledge sources to enrich context and maximise translation quality
Customisable Workflow
Flexible content processing with intelligent filtering, automated pre/post-translation adaptations, and customisable workflow steps. Handle tricky formats, automate recurring tasks, or enhance your processing with AI Agents - we can compose powerful processing for your most demanding content.
Project Management
Dedicated project management with clear timelines, progress tracking, and seamless communication throughout the translation process - or access to these features should you wish to manage your own teams
Asset Integration
Systematic knowledge is captured throughout the translation process, building and maintaining glossaries, knowledge graphs, and translation memories that create a competitive moat difficult for others to replicate
Format Flexibility
Support for over 100 file formats - from print-ready InDesign and FrameMaker to structured XML-based DITA. Smart filtering reduces unnecessary translation volume while preserving structural integrity
